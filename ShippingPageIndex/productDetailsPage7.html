Ship your baggage anywhere in the world with us. Save yourself time, stress and money when it comes to luggage delivery.

send bags ahead to where they need to go as quickly and as efficiently as possible.

Why My Baggage?
My Baggage makes transporting your luggage
overseas an effortless process. Our easy to use
online booking system is available 24/7.

Here’s what you can expect when booking with
My Baggage:

- Low cost luggage shipping to over 200
countries worldwide.

- Convenient door to door collection and
delivery services.

- Fast transit times with express services
available on many routes.

- Full online luggage tracking with SMS and
email notifications.


- Dedicated customer support via phone,
email and live chat.
